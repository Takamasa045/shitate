# Lexicon / Clothing / 十二単・平安装束

平安時代の貴族装束。女性の「十二単（正式名: 女房装束・五衣唐衣裳）」と男性の「束帯」「直衣」。

---

## #heian-court

**用途**: 平安貴族の正式装束（女性）。宮廷シーン・和歌文化・雅な対話カットに。

**プロンプト断片**:
```
heian period japanese court lady in twelve-layered kimono (juni-hitoe),
multiple silk layers visible at sleeves and hem in graduated colors,
red outer karaginu jacket, trailing white mo apron-train,
long straight black hair flowing down past the waist unbound,
white powdered face, faint vermilion on lips,
classical yamato-e aesthetic
```

## #sokutai

**用途**: 平安貴族の正式装束（男性）。朝廷・儀式・摂関家シーンに。

**プロンプト断片**:
```
heian period japanese nobleman in sokutai formal court attire,
black silk outer robe (ho), wide white trousers (sashinuki),
tall black lacquered cap (kanmuri) with tail (ei),
shaku tablet held in hand, serious composed expression,
classical yamato-e aesthetic
```
