## #samurai-residence

**用途**: 武家屋敷の内部。対話・謀議・決断のシーンに。

**プロンプト断片**:
```
japanese samurai residence interior, tatami mat floor, shoji paper screens,
simple wooden beams, low table, tokonoma alcove with hanging scroll,
restrained decoration, quiet wooden architecture,
edo or nanboku-cho period minka aesthetic
```

## #heian-residence

**用途**: 平安貴族の邸宅。宮廷シーン・雅な対話に。

**プロンプト断片**:
```
heian period japanese aristocratic residence, shinden-zukuri architecture,
raised wooden floor with bamboo blinds (sudare), misu screens,
pale wood and white walls, painted sliding screens with yamato-e scenes,
formal garden visible through open eaves,
classical kamakura emakimono cutaway perspective
```
